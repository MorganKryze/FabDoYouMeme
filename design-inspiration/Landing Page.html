How to play

Shuffle. Deal. Laugh. Repeat.

Three steps, one code, zero friction. Your friends don't sign up — they just show up.

♠ Step I
1

Host a room

Pick a game, pick a pack, get a 4-letter room code. Everything runs on your box — no cloud in the loop.

♥ Step II
2

Friends pile in

They tap the code on their phone. No download, no account, no tracking. Guests get a seat at the table.

♦ Step III
3

Caption & vote

Round by round. The best punchline wins the hand. Scoring, timers, and reconnects are on the house.

The small print (but fun)

Yours. Not theirs.

Most party platforms turn your laughter into somebody's training data. This one doesn't — it's GPLv3, self-hosted, and boring where it counts.

♠

Self-hosted, one command

One Docker Compose stack. Old laptop, home server, €5 VPS — it runs anywhere you run containers.

♥

Invite-only by design

No public signup. Single-use invite tokens, optional email restriction. You decide who sits at your table.

♦

Magic-link auth

No passwords to leak or rotate. Tokens are SHA-256 hashed in the DB, single-use, expire in 15 minutes.

♣

GDPR-ready

Consent capture, one-click data export, admin-driven erasure with sentinel-UUID anonymisation. Retention windows built in.

★

Multi-game platform

Meme captioning is the first handler; trivia, drawing duels, pairs, quickfire slot in as plugins. No schema churn.

⚙

Read every line

GPLv3. Go backend, SvelteKit frontend. Fork it, rebrand it, rewire it — just don't lock it back up.
